Case Study: Mobile First Transformation

Bridging the gap between London's automotive heritage and the modern digital concierge expectation.

Role: UX & UI Designer
About

Your Gateway to Luxury Car Rentals

Overview

Miles & Miles is one of London's oldest luxury car rental services, established in 1953. This project transformed their outdated web-only booking system into a modern, mobile-first concierge experience designed for high-net-worth individuals.

Target Audience: London-based professionals, tourists & event planners seeking premium vehicles.

Objective: Reduce mobile drop-off by 40% and create a booking flow under 90 seconds.

Platform: Mobile App

The Problem

Despite a premium physical fleet, Miles & Miles' digital presence was causing significant drop-off. High-net-worth users were abandoning bookings due to cumbersome web forms and a lack of mobile-specific logistics tracking.

The Goal

Create a mobile experience that matches the "concierge-level" physical service London's elite have come to expect since 1953 — reducing booking time from 6+ minutes to under 90 seconds.

Pain Points

1

Limited Digital Access

No dedicated mobile app. Users were forced to navigate a desktop-optimised website on their phones, leading to poor usability.

2

Lack of Self-Service Options

Booking still required phone calls during business hours. Clients expected instant, 24/7 digital booking capabilities.

3

Inconvenient Pickup Locations

No real-time tracking for car delivery or pickup. Users had no visibility into where their vehicle was or when it would arrive.

4

Missing Personalisation

The system didn't remember user preferences or past bookings. Every rental felt like a first-time experience.
