About Me

From economics to espresso
to experience design

Originally from Türkiye, my path to design has been anything but linear. After graduating in Economics in 2018, I moved to London for an Erasmus+ internship as a Financial Business Analyst, consulting for startups and falling in love with building things from zero.

That energy led me to found and run my own ☕ coffee shop in London ▶ (2019-2020) during one of the toughest periods for small businesses. It was a masterclass in resilience, empathy, and understanding what people actually need, not what they say they need.

Since 2020, I've channelled that entrepreneurial instinct into tech as a self-employed consultant, specialising in user journey mapping, pain-point diagnosis, and rapid prototyping, all supercharged with AI integration for smarter, human-centric products.
